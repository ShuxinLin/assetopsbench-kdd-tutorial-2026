Building Reliable Industrial Agents with MCP:
A Hands-on AssetOpsBench Tutorial for AI-Driven Operations

2026 KDD Lecture-style / Hands-on Tutorial

Day, Month DD, 2026   0:00 AM – 0:00 PM

Location: Venue, City, Country

Abstract

First abstract paragraph. Introduce the problem: AI agents and LLMs are increasingly applied to industrial asset operations — predictive maintenance, anomaly detection, fault diagnosis, work-order reasoning — yet evaluating their reliability in these high-stakes settings remains an open challenge.

Second abstract paragraph. Introduce AssetOpsBench: what it measures, the tasks and data it covers, the evaluation methodology, and what attendees will learn. Summarize the takeaways and the hands-on component of the tutorial.
